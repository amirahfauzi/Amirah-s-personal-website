My Experience: Volunteering at Zoo Negara


Our volunteering bracelet

This is my first volunteering ever on 2018. It was a really great and new experience
for me. I could say I enjoyed every moment throughout the volunteering session. At that
time I was only 17 years old. At first, I was really nervous because I have to do the volunteering
with strangers that I met there. However, all of them were so nice to me and they
made me feel comfortable to be myself. Honestly, I wish to volunteer again at
the Zoo Negara if I had the chance someday. I would recommend 100% this to anyone
who has the thought to volunteer there.
